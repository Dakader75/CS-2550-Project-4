WHY IV THERAPY?

Because our bodies need vitamins and other essential nutrients in order to function properly, ensuring that the body has access to essential nutrients is an important part of maintaining optimal well-being. IV therapy bypasses the digestive system, resulting in 100% absorption. Vitamins that are taken as oral supplements have varied and significantly lower percentage of absorption. IV infusions are so beneficial because these vitamins and other nutrients are delivered rapidly, helping you feel better almost immediately after the infusion.
